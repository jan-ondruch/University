What is Sporterizer?

Sporterizer is a modern sports application, that utilizes real-time data to help coaches train better. It was developed as a bachelor thesis by Jan Ondruch, under a supervision from Ing. Vítězslav Beran, Ph.D.. Technology, as well as the idea for the project belongs to Sewio Networks s.r.o.
