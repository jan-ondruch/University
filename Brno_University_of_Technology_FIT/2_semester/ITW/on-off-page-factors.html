On-Page / Off-Page Factors

  • Title-tag: Adding a relevant title tag shorter than 55 characters ensures proper visibility in website browsers and strongly increases likelihood, that exactly this site will be found using keywords that user typed into "search" bar. SEO is responsible for this behavior.
  • Image-alt-text: SEO optimalization, adding relevant text to a product increases possibility of finding this site.
  • Description: Similar to the first optimalization.
  • HTML document structure: Adding proper tags and keeping neat structure works just right in your favour, since it's another element SEO will look for.
  • Simple content & responsible server: Increase loading speed of website.
  • Locality of Company: Known locality of company targets "more-relevant" audience.
